My Journey

Over the course of delivering a nine-slide presentation on Exploring the Benefits of a Tech Career, I gained a deeper understanding of the industry and how technology shapes our lives. The presentation gave a broad overview of opportunities in tech, from software development to data science, cybersecurity, AI, and more.

What stood out most was the huge variety of career options. Technology offers many disciplines to suit different skills and personalities. Creative minds may enjoy front-end or UX design, while analytical thinkers might lean toward data science or machine learning. This diversity makes the industry dynamic and inclusive.

CSS Journey

Styling the Web with Creativity

Growing My Understanding of CSS

As of this morning, Friday, 15 August 2025, my understanding of CSS (Cascading Style Sheets) has grown significantly. At first glance, CSS seemed like nothing more than a way to add color and adjust fonts, but I now recognize it as a powerful tool that gives websites their identity and personality.

Separation of Content and Design

One of the key aspects I have learned is that CSS allows for the separation of content from design. HTML is responsible for the structure and content of a webpage — things like headings, paragraphs, images, and links.
